# brand-spec.md

## Project
Redesign prototype for Piekarnia Bindrich website

## Brand name
Piekarnia Bindrich

## Brand type
Local bakery and confectionery brand

## Location
Piekary Śląskie, Poland

## Core brand facts
- Bakery products are baked on natural sourdough.
- Products are made without artificial improvers and preservatives.
- The brand combines traditional recipes with modern technological infrastructure.
- Production is controlled at every stage to ensure quality.
- The company emphasizes authenticity, taste, nutritional value, and trust.
- Main categories presented on the site: baked goods, żur, cakes, artisanal ice cream.
- The company communicates tradition, craftsmanship, and modern quality standards.

## Brand personality
- Traditional but not old-fashioned
- Natural
- Trustworthy
- Local
- Warm
- Premium craft
- Family-friendly
- Authentic
- Appetizing

## Visual direction goals
- Avoid generic SaaS / startup aesthetics
- Avoid purple gradients, tech glow, emoji icons, AI-slop cards
- Avoid over-corporate or sterile minimalism
- Focus on premium food brand feel
- Warm editorial composition
- High-quality food photography direction
- Strong tactile atmosphere: crust, flour, grain, dough, heat, craft
- Use subtle animation, not flashy effects

## Suggested color mood
- Warm cream / flour / parchment backgrounds
- Deep brown / charcoal typography
- Optional muted burgundy, dark wheat gold, or forest-green accent
- Overall low-saturation premium palette

## Suggested typography mood
- Editorial display serif or refined humanist display for headings
- Clean readable sans or soft serif for body
- Must feel culinary, crafted, premium, local
- Not a tech product font system

## UX goals
- Communicate quality within first screen
- Help user quickly understand product range
- Make store-finder / points of sale easy to access
- Build trust through brand story and production values
- Prepare structure for future promotions / seasonal products

## Primary sections for prototype
1. Hero with value proposition
2. Quality pillars / brand differentiators
3. Product category showcase
4. Bestsellers or seasonal highlights
5. Store locator / city-based points of sale
6. Brand story / tradition + modern production
7. Contact + social links

## Reactive components recommended
- Category switcher with animated preview
- Store locator with city filter
- Seasonal featured product carousel
- Story timeline / trust section
- Sticky header with CTA
- Mobile-friendly accordion for locations

## Motion guidance
- Slow, elegant reveal-on-scroll
- Soft parallax on hero photography
- Hover transitions for product cards
- Animated counters or map/store indicators only if tasteful
- Motion should feel artisanal and premium, not playful-tech

## Constraints
- Must be mobile-friendly
- Should work as a single-file HTML prototype first
- Should be suitable for later rewrite in React / Next.js
- Should preserve local-business credibility
- Should use real or realistic bakery imagery direction
